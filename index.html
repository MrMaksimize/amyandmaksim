The Story

The specific where's, how's and why's aren't that important (and probably not too interesting to people who are not either of them). They were best friends for years in Chicago before either of them realized they had been crazy about each other for most of that time.

He visited her in the hospital every day and her family could not convince her that his feelings for her were anything other than platonic.

They made a list of places to travel to together as buddies before they realized they wanted to go there as husband and wife.

She cried when she told him she had feelings for him, certain she was losing her best friend. He convinced her otherwise.

He told her she'd never learn how to spell "Pecherskiy" when she first met him. Now she's taking it as her last name.

He's the kindest, most selfless person she knows. And the dorkiest.

He feels the same way about her.

About Cafe Ba Ba Reeba

Cafe Ba-Ba-Reeba! is a premier Spanish tapas restaurant located in Chicago. Cafe Ba-Ba-Reeba! is known for Tapas, the hot & cold appetizers of Spain; Paella, a typical Valencian rice dish served with vegetables and meat or seafood; and Sangria, Spain's famous wine "fiesta" drink made from fruit, wine and liquors.
